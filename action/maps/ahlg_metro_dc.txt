Metro - Hunted
==============

VIP's from Western Dynamics need to escape assassins and 
reach safety. They need to reach Western Dynamics Corporate HQ,
using the backdoor. The backdoor is at the end of a dark alley. 
Head for the yellow works hut and then through the gate in the 
Wooden fence to reach it. 

Body Guards have been called in to protect the VIPs from the
would be assassins. 

If a VIP reaches safety, Western Dynamics get 2 points. 
If the assassins kill a VIP, they will get 1 point.

BodyGuards and Assassins have infinite lives and a 5 second 
Respawn Delay.
