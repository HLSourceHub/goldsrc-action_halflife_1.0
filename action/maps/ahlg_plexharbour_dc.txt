Plexharbour  - Assassination
============================

The Usual Suspects must get aboard the ship in the Harbour
and kill the account. 
 
The accountant is hiding in the stairway near the bridge.

The Ship's Crew get infinite lives but have a delay in re-spawning.

The Usual Suspects get 3 lives each.

The Usual Suspects must kill the accountant to win. 

The Ship's Crew must eliminate all of the Usual Suspects to win.
