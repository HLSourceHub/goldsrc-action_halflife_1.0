Engholm - Assassination
=======================

Jeff is the target of a Mafia hit team. The Professionals are coming
to clean him. Only his Killers can save him. 

Jeff is hiding out in a room on the roof. 

The Killers get infinite lives.

The Professionals get 3 lives each. 

The Professionals must eliminate Jeff to win.

The Killers must eliminate the entire hit team to win.

Original Map Author : Dirk Schraeer (aka banana)
