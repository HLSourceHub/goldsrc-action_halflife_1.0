BASICS:
Type        : Akimbo 9mm semi-auto pistols
Firing modes: Semi-Auto/full-Auto
Ammo loadout: 30 rounds/2 clips, 2 extra clips
Firing rate : Medium/fast
Damage      : Low

TACTICAL/COMMENTS:
Blessed with a high ammo count and average damage, akimbo light pistols are a good selection for many players. There is nothing more stylish than rushing an enemy, both guns blazing. Additionally, the Berettas can be equipped with silencers making them perfect for infiltration.

AS SEEN IN:
Akimbo pistols have their place in every movie made by John Woo like Hard Boiled and The Killer. For the silenced akimbo crowd, check out Ghost Dog or Boondock Saints.
