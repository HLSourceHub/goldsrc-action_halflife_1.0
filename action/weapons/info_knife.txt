BASICS:
Type        : Throwing knives
Firing modes: Slash/throw
Ammo loadout: 10 knives
Firing rate : ---/Medium
Damage      : High

TACTICAL/COMMENTS:
Those who like stealth will love the knives. They are very powerful and long ranged but arc when thrown, making them a weapon for the highly-skilled. When the enemy is close enough you can easily stab them. Thrown knives can also be recollected and used again. Best used with the stealth slippers or bandolier.

AS SEEN IN:
To see how deadly throwing knives can be, simply watch Desperado.
