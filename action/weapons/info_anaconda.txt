BASICS:
Type        : .44 Magnum Revolver
Firing modes: Single shot, drill
Ammo loadout: 6 rounds/drum, 2 extra drums
Firing rate : Slow/medium
Damage      : High

TACTICAL/COMMENTS:
Having a good stopping power together with great penetration, the .44 Magnum revolver is used best on close quarters even though it is still very accurate in medium-ranged fire fights. It is even more deadly when used together with a laser sight, making it nearly 100% accurate.

AS SEEN IN:
The 44. Magnum revolver is an icon of action movies and is featured in the Dirty Harry series and The Long Kiss Goodnight.
